Frequently Asked Questions

How quickly can you start cleaning?

We can typically schedule your first cleaning within 48-72 hours of your request. For emergency or same-day services, please call us directly at (555) 123-4567.

Are your cleaning products safe for children and pets?

Absolutely. We use only eco-friendly, non-toxic, and biodegradable cleaning products that are safe for children, pets, and those with allergies or sensitivities.

Do I need to be home during the cleaning?

No, many of our clients provide us with access to their homes or offices. All our staff are thoroughly vetted, bonded, and insured for your peace of mind.

What's included in your satisfaction guarantee?

If you're not completely satisfied with any aspect of our service, let us know within 24 hours and we'll return to re-clean the area at no additional cost.
